Apples at first sight

Our love story began playing the infamous, wild card game Apples to Apples! As the game progressed and hilarious comparisons were made, our friends noticed a predictable pattern...Natalie was consistently guessing Joey’s cards to the point of everyone thinking they had devised a scheme. It was the first time they had met, but it was as if they had known each other long before. The game ended with a sweeping victory for Joey and the start of a new season for both! We're truly blessed by the miracles God has performed in our lives, and feel one of the most cherished, is being brought together.
